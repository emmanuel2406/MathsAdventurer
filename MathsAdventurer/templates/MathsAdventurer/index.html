Our mission

Maths Adventurer is a free South African based resource and training portal for all learners wanting to compete in the esteemed Maths Olympiads. Our aim is to build a path for talented learners no matter their background. If you think you have what it takes please register now to join us on this incredible journey of problem-solving.

What this platform offers

  • Links to various websites for info, registration and past papers
  • Keep track of all the national (and international) competitions
  • Resources categorised by topic and difficulty
  • Calendar for competition dates

Why register?

  1. Keep track of competitions with a watchlist
  2. Plan goals for future competitions
  3. Keep track of past performances
  4. Be sent exclusive invitations to training events
  5. And more

COMPETE

We aim to instill confidence in your own ability and create a stress-free mindset approaching any competition regardless of context.

EXPLORE

We aim to cultivate ambition and inspire passion for the subject. Learning can be a journey full of excitement and surprises.

REPEAT

We aim to encourage perseverance and resilience through failure. "Success is not final. Failure is not fatal."
